# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Fashion-focused Arabic and English-speaking shoppers who appreciate clean, modern, and highly-organized online shopping interfaces. They look for quick navigation, clear deals, and high-quality apparel photography.

## Product Purpose
A highly polished, clean, and modern headless e-commerce storefront for fashion and apparel. It connects to a Shopify backend via the Storefront API, displaying products in a high-contrast modular grid that mimics premium retail platforms, with smooth animations and instant loading.

## Brand Personality
**Clean, Modern Minimalist**:
- High contrast, pure white backgrounds, and breathable whitespace.
- Professional, trustworthy, and visually structured.
- Bold promotional typography combined with clean, isolated product visuals.
- Voice is direct, active, and customer-centric.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered layouts with overlapping textured grids or noisy backgrounds.
- Brutalist or technical monospace-only designs.
- Low-contrast light gray text on cream backdrops.

## Design Principles
1. **Uncluttered breathing room**: Prioritize generous margins and whitespace to highlight product photography and reduce cognitive load.
2. **Clear visual indicators**: Use highly visible colors (like vibrant rose red) to flag promotions, sale progression, active states, and countdowns.
3. **Structured hierarchy**: Lead with bold geometric headings, clean legible details, and small standard microcopy for product specifications.
4. **Familiar conventions**: Utilize standard e-commerce grid cards, heart buttons for wishlists, and search inputs to make the shopping process intuitive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Maintain strong contrast ratios (≥4.5:1) for text elements against pure white or light gray backgrounds.
- Fully support both Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR) layout switching.
- Incorporate clear state outlines or borders on focusable inputs.
- Ensure transitions respect users with motion sensitivity settings.
