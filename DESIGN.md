# Design System: Clean Minimalist Storefront

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design system focuses on a **Modern, Minimalist, and Clean** e-commerce look. It uses pure white surfaces, high-contrast typography, very light gray boundaries, and spacious layouts. The atmosphere is professional, polished, and structured to highlight product images and promotional events like flash sales.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)**: Primary background. Used for pages, headers, product cards, and navigation.
* **Light Gray (`#F7F8FA`)**: Secondary background. Used for product image wrappers and section backgrounds.
* **Dark Navy/Black (`#1A1C23`)**: Primary accent. Used for text headings, active filters, and primary CTA buttons.
* **Rose Red (`#E63946`)**: Secondary accent. Used for countdown timers, sale progress bars, active icons, and discount labels.
* **Medium Gray (`#9E9E9E`)**: Subdued text and inactive elements. Used for original prices, ratings, and borders.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Primary Headings**: Bold to Extra Bold geometric sans-serif (e.g., *Poppins* or *Urbanist*), used for promotional headlines and section headings.
* **Product Details**: Clean, regular to medium sans-serif. Used for product names, pricing numbers, and descriptions.
* **Technical Labels & Microcopy**: Small, regular sans-serif for ratings, sold counts, and footer utility text.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Product Cards**: White background container with a very subtle drop shadow on hover. The top 65% is a light gray `#F7F8FA` image block with a floating circular wishlist (heart) button in the top right.
* **Pill Buttons**: Capsule/pill shape with fully rounded corners. Active state is solid dark navy (`#1A1C23`) with white text; inactive state is white background with `#E2E8F0` border and gray text.
* **Search Bar**: Centered header search bar with rounded corners, category selector dropdown on the left, and search icon on the right.
* **Countdown Badges**: Circular rose-red badges (`#E63946`) showing hours, minutes, and seconds in white text, separated by bold colons.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Modular Grid**: Structured columns (4 columns on desktop, 2 on mobile) separated by clean spacing, avoiding explicit borders.
* **Hero Split**: Promotional banner with bold typography on the left and floating, layered product layouts on the right.
* **Horizontal Filter Chips**: Row of capsule elements for instant collection filtering.
